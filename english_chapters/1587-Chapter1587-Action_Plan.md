Several people surrounding a table in a small room in the backyard of the Huang Family Medicine Shop remained silent, staring deadly at the crooked floor plan drawn on the rough-edged paper.

In the shop front outside, although it was already night, it was still very lively. There were people entering and leaving to see doctors and buy medicine. Voices came faintly.

Thick curtains hung on the windows, and disciples were on guard outside. They could discuss the details of the next action with confidence and boldness.

"Zhongjun, add another candle." Sima Qiudao broke the silence.

Several "Australian foreign candles" had already been lit in the room. Although not as "bright as day" as the gas lamps in the shop front, it was considered very bright.

If we act rashly, I'm afraid we'll have to knaw on hard bones!" Wanli Feng, after all, had seasoned Jianghu experience and pointed out the key to the problem in one sentence.

Even if we can mix into the square, once we start, the losses won't be small, and I'm afraid it will be difficult to retreat completely."

The floor plan in front of them was drawn by combining the intelligence they had scouted themselves these days and the intelligence sent by Old Man Shi.

It looked like there was simply no opportunity to exploit. Even in the square, there were hundreds of guards in "bright helmets and armor" alone. Old Man Shi's intelligence said that there must be many plainclothes personnel on the scene.

Although Old Man Shi's intelligence said clearly that no special personnel would be set up to interrogate in the square at that time, and superficially entry and exit were free, in reality, it was loose on the outside and tight on the inside. Any slight abnormality would lead to arrest.

As for entering the gymnasium, it was even more impossible. The intelligence showed that everyone entering the gymnasium at that time had to hold a ticket. Those without tickets were categorically not allowed to enter. Moreover, the internal defenses were stricter, and even Old Man Shi did not have detailed intelligence.

Looking at this map, the four people all sucked in a breath of cold air. Originally thought that entering the gymnasium to assassinate was seeking one's own death, now it seemed that even taking action in the square was a narrow escape from death.

Although there were many crowds and the Bandits' firearms could not function effectively, they had the advantage of numbers. No matter how great one's own skills were, there was no chance of winning one against ten.
